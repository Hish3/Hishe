You can upgrade the right of use by paying an additional fee. I can post the final product with commission watermark and lower resolution on my page. (If this is a private commission, please inform me. Or alternatively you can pay for the copyright license so i don't have the right anymore to use the artwork)

2. After agreement on price, I will proceed to do the sketch, and send it to you. Full payment after the sketch approved

3. I will proceed with the lineart and show it to you.

4. I will do the base color and rendering (this stage takes a bit longer)

5. I will send you the final product via Google Drive link 2x revision in before lineart

6. Major revision will be charged (25k/revision)

7. Processing time is carried out for roughly 15 days for final render and 3-5 days for sketch (can change depending on the situation)

8. No rush. Additional fee for rush (25k/days from my initial time to sketch or render)

9. Canvas size 3508x2480px (roughly A4)

10. Final file type you will receive is a PNG.
